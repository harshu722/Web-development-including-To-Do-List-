"Whispers of the Moonlit Owl"

In the stillness of the night, on a moonlit tree so high, An owl perches gracefully, with feathers soft and sly. Its eyes, like orbs of wisdom, pierce through the darkness deep, A sentinel of twilight, where secrets silently creep. A ghostly figure, haunting, as it surveys the land, Wings spread wide, a silent glide, on nature's unseen hand. With a hoot so hauntingly melodic, it calls out to the moon, Echoes through the silent forest, a lullaby-like tune. Majestic in its silence, it's a guardian of the night, A hunter wise, a master of disguise, in the pale moon's gentle light. Its presence, a symbol of mystery, a creature of the wise, Whispering tales of ancient lore, beneath the starry skies. Oh, owl upon the tree, with your enigmatic grace, You paint a portrait in the night, a guardian of this place. Through the shifting shadows, you hold the secrets dear, A sentinel of moonlit dreams, forever perched up here.

Written by k.saharsh
